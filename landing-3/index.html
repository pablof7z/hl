What makes it different

Built for depth, not engagement metrics.

Every feature is designed around one goal: the best conversations you've ever had about the things you consume.

🎯

The artifact is the hero

Everything organizes around the source content — not random posts or hot takes. A book, podcast, or article is the center. Highlights and discussions exist in service of that content.

🔒

Private by default

Your communities are yours. Invite-only, encrypted when needed, and never algorithmically surfaced. Share publicly when you choose to — not because a platform decided for you.

🔀

Highlights travel between circles

Share the same article to your book club and your work group. Each community discusses independently — but you see all conversations in one unified view.

✨

AI-suggested highlights

Drop any URL and Highlighter surfaces the most discussion-worthy passages. Accept them, edit them, or choose your own. The AI works for you, not the other way around.

Built on Nostr

You own your identity. You own your data. For real.

Highlighter is built on the Nostr protocol — an open, decentralized network. No corporate accounts. No platform lock-in. Your highlights, your communities, your conversations belong to you.

  • ✓ Your keys, your identity. No email/password accounts. Login with your Nostr key — it works across the entire ecosystem.
  • ✓ Portable communities. Your groups can move between relays. If you don't like us, take your community somewhere else. Seriously.
  • ✓ Exportable everything. Highlights, comments, memberships — standard Nostr events you can use in any compatible client.
  • ✓ No data mining. We don't sell your reading habits, your discussions, or your social graph. Ever.

🔑 Your Keys

One cryptographic identity across all of Nostr. No more creating accounts on every new platform. Your identity is yours — forever.

🌐 Open Protocol

Built on NIP-29 relay-based groups. Anyone can build compatible clients. The protocol is open and the community is growing.

🚪 No Lock-in

Don't like us? Export your data, move your community to another relay, and keep going. We earn your loyalty by being good — not by trapping you.
