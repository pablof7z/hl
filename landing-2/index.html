Built different

You own everything. We mean it.

Highlighter is built on Nostr — an open protocol, not a company's API. Here's what that means for you:

Your identity is yours

No email/password account. You sign in with cryptographic keys you control. Nobody — not even us — can lock you out or delete your account.

Your data goes where you go

Every highlight, every comment, every community membership is a standard Nostr event. Export it. Move it. Use it in any compatible app. There's no walled garden.

Communities aren't trapped

Your book club is a real NIP-29 group on a relay. If you don't like our relay, move it. The community belongs to its members, not to our servers.

Privacy that's structural

Private communities use protocol-level privacy — only authenticated members can read the content. We don't mine your data. We don't sell your reading habits. We can't.

If you don't care about any of this right now, that's fine. It works like any other app. But when a platform you love eventually shuts down, changes terms, or sells to someone you don't trust — you'll be glad this one was built different.
