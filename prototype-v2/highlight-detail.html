Bob's note

This one stopped me cold. We spend so much time reacting — to email, to Slack, to whatever's urgent — but the real leverage is in creation. In choosing what to build next rather than responding to what just happened. Kahneman frames it as the difference between System 1 reacting and System 2 deciding. This quote is the System 2 manifesto.
