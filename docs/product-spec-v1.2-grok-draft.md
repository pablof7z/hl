This is a **fundamental pivot** from the previous "curated Edition-first" public model. We are now building **Highlighter as a network of small, trusted, user-created communities** — exactly as described. The product becomes a **private-first highlight-sharing layer for friend groups and small circles**, with seamless cross-community sharing and automatic union of discussions.

This model directly solves the original traction problems David King called out: no more forcing a broad "anyone can post anything" network. Instead, **immediate value exists the moment you invite 5–10 friends**. Growth is purely organic and product-led because every user gets a useful, high-signal experience in their own small trusted circle from day one.

## 1. Core Vision (Refined for Community-First)

Highlighter is **the social layer for humanity's best ideas inside your trusted circles**.

- You and your friends create private communities (think "Book Club 2026", "Deep Tech Ideas", "Philosophy Fridays", or even "Our Family Highlights").
- Highlights are the atomic unit shared inside those communities.
- The same highlight can be cross-posted to multiple communities you belong to.
- All comments, remixes, and discussions from every community automatically **union** into one global thread for that highlight (visible to you wherever you posted it).
- Public view is **read-only** (lurkers can discover and get hooked, but must be invited to participate).

**2026 differentiator**: In the AI content explosion, Highlighter becomes the place where small groups of smart friends turn passive consumption into shared, high-signal conversations — without ever needing the entire platform to be huge.

## 2. Key Principles (Aligned with Shutdown Note + Feedback)

- **Community-first, not platform-first**: Small circles win. Communities naturally stay small and high-trust (the magic commenters loved in David's original town-halls).
- **Invite-only by default** → zero cold-start problem.
- **Immediate value** → you don't need network effects across the whole app.
- **Cross-community union** → creates lightweight network effects without forcing public everything.
- **Read-only public layer** → discovery and virality without spam.
- AI still augments (smart suggest, synthesis, discussion facilitation) but **never replaces** human taste in small trusted groups.

## 3. Core Features (MVP Built Around Communities)

### Phase 1 MVP (Launch Q3 2026)

- **Community Creation & Management**
  - One-tap "Create Community" (invite-only by default).
  - Name, description, cover image, optional rules/moderators.
  - Invite via link, email, phone, X/LinkedIn, or QR code (frictionless).
  - Size tends naturally toward 8–50 people (sweet spot for deep discussion).

- **Highlight Sharing Inside Communities**
  - Universal capture (browser extension, mobile, Kindle, Spotify, YouTube, PDFs, etc.).
  - Share a highlight → choose **one or multiple communities** you're in.
  - AI Smart Suggest still helps surface best excerpts.

- **Unified Discussion Layer**
  - Every highlight has **one canonical discussion thread**.
  - Comments from *all* communities where it was shared automatically union together (you see the full conversation no matter which group you're viewing it from).
  - Threaded replies, remixes, reactions, and AI co-host (suggests questions, summarizes side debates).

- **Public Read-Only View**
  - Any highlight shared publicly (opt-in per highlight or per community) is visible to the world as beautiful cards.
  - No comments or participation unless invited to a community that contains it.
  - Great for SEO and organic discovery ("What everyone is highlighting about X").

- **Personal Vault**
  - Your private library across all communities + AI synthesis ("Show me everything my circles highlighted about decision-making").

### Phase 2 (Q4 2026+)

- Creator-owned communities (authors can create their own and invite fans).
- Advanced cross-community discovery (with permission): "See highlights from communities similar to mine."
- Nostr/decentralized mode for power users.
- Voice mode + multimodal highlights.
- Enterprise "Internal Team Communities" version.

## 4. Growth Strategy: Product = Marketing Engine

This Community-First model **makes the product an exponentially better growth engine** than the previous Edition-first approach.

| Growth Loop | How It Works in Community-First | Why It Compounds |
|---|---|---|
| **Invite → Instant Value** | You create a community → invite 5 friends → everyone immediately starts sharing highlights from whatever they're reading/watching. No waiting for critical mass. | Pure viral coefficient > 1.5 expected. Users invite because they get value on day 1. |
| **Highlight → Cross-Post → Union** | Share one highlight to 3 different friend groups → all discussions merge automatically. Friends in different groups discover each other's takes. | Creates natural "pull" for more invites ("You should join this other group too"). |
| **Public Read-Only Virality** | A highlight from a private community is made public → outsiders see beautiful card + "Join a community discussing this" CTA. | Free acquisition channel without opening the floodgates. |
| **Multi-Community Network Effect** | One person belongs to 4–5 communities → their highlights flow across them → friends see activity and want in. | Lightweight global graph emerges organically. |
| **Creator & Influencer Flywheel** | Thought leaders create their own community → invite core fans → fans cross-post highlights to their own friend groups. | Creators become distribution partners. |

### Phased Rollout

- **Launch**: Every new user starts by creating or joining one community. Browser extension prompts "Share this highlight with your friends' community?"
- **No paid ads needed**: 70–80%+ of growth expected from invites + public highlight cards.
