✦

The artifact is the hero

Every conversation lives under the book, episode, or article — not lost in a timeline. Come back to it in a week. Still there, still organized.

✦

Highlights, not hot takes

The atomic unit is an excerpt from something real — not a hot take from nothing. Conversations start grounded in the actual content.

✦

Private by default

Your group's conversations belong to your group. Nothing is public unless you choose. No one can accidentally see your discussion about chapter 7.

✦

Share one thing to multiple circles

Post the same article to your book club and your work crew simultaneously. Each group has its own conversation. Your personal vault collects everything.

✦

Browser extension + mobile capture

One click from any webpage. Snap a Kindle highlight. Share a podcast moment. Getting things into Highlighter is faster than texting a link.

✦

Your personal vault

Every highlight you've ever made, searchable and yours forever — across all your groups. Never lose something you marked as worth keeping.
